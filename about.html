Our Story

Founded on Shared Experience, Built on Trust

Inoa & Melenciano Services was born from a simple truth: navigating American systems—from buying a home to filing taxes to starting a business—is complicated for everyone, but especially challenging when you're doing it in a second language, far from your homeland.

Co-founders Eduardo Inoa and Ader Melenciano understand this firsthand. As members of the Dominican diaspora, they experienced the frustration of miscommunication, the fear of making costly mistakes, and the isolation of not knowing who to trust. They saw family members, friends, and community members struggle with the same barriers.

So they decided to build a solution: a bilingual, licensed, professional service hub that combines real estate, tax preparation, credit repair, immigration support, business registration, and notary services—all under one roof, all delivered with cultural understanding and genuine care.

Today, Inoa & Melenciano Services serves thousands of Hispanic families across Massachusetts and beyond, helping them achieve homeownership, build businesses, establish credit, complete immigration processes, and invest in their homeland through our exclusive Paraíso Inmobiliario platform.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To deliver accessible, professional, and trustworthy real estate, financial, and business services that empower individuals and families—especially within the Hispanic and Dominican communities—to build wealth, navigate complex systems, invest with confidence, and achieve lasting financial security.

Vision

To become the leading Hispanic service agency in the United States and the trusted bridge connecting U.S.-based families to Dominican Republic real estate investments—creating a legacy of homeownership, entrepreneurship, and generational wealth.

How We're Different

We don't just translate services—we culturally adapt them. We don't just process paperwork—we build relationships. We don't just close deals—we celebrate your success as our own. Our Journey

2018

The Beginning

Eduardo and Ader launch tax preparation and credit repair services for the Hispanic community in Lawrence, MA. Focus: helping families understand the American tax system.

2020

Real Estate Partnership

Ader obtains REALTOR® license and joins Century 21 North East / The Fermin Group. Begin integrating real estate with tax and credit services for first-time homebuyers.

2021

Service Expansion

Add immigration form preparation, business registration, and notary public services. Establish Newfed Mortgage partnership for seamless financing coordination.

2023

Paraíso Inmobiliario Launch

Launch exclusive Dominican Republic real estate investment platform connecting U.S.-based diaspora with premium properties in their homeland.

2024

Milestone Achievement

Serve 3,000+ clients across 8 service categories. Establish reputation as premier Hispanic service agency in Greater Boston area.

2025+

Future Growth

Expanding digital tools, launching client portal, developing referral program, and building scalable systems to serve even more families across New England.
