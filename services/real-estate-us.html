Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the home buying process take?

Typical timelines range from 30–60 days from offer acceptance to closing. Getting pre-approved early helps move faster.

Do I need a mortgage pre-approval before touring?

It’s strongly recommended. Pre-approval clarifies budget and strengthens your offers in competitive markets.

Do you work with first-time buyers?

Yes. We guide first-time buyers through programs, down payment options, and step-by-step education (ES/EN).

How are real estate agent fees paid?

In many residential transactions, the seller pays listing and buyer agent commissions. We’ll confirm specifics for your situation.
