Immigration Form Assistance

Bilingual USCIS form preparation—citizenship, green cards, work permits & family petitions

Administrative Assistance Only: We help complete USCIS forms and organize documents. We are not immigration attorneys. For complex cases or legal representation, consult a licensed attorney.

Common Forms We Help With

N-400 Citizenship

U.S. citizenship application for green card holders (5+ years or 3 if married to citizen)

I-130 Family Petition

Sponsoring family members—spouse, parents, children, siblings

I-485 Green Card

Adjustment of status to permanent residence while in U.S.

I-765 Work Permit

Employment authorization (EAD) for temporary work permission

I-90 Renewal

Green card renewal or replacement (expired/lost/damaged)

I-131 Travel Permit

Re-entry permit or advance parole for international travel

How We Help

1

Consultation

Assess eligibility, explain requirements, provide document checklist. 30-45 min

2

Form Completion

Complete USCIS form bilingually, organize documents, verify accuracy. 1-2 hours

3

Filing & Support

Assemble package, file with USCIS, track case, provide interview prep. Ongoing

Typical Documents Needed

Identity & Status

  • Passport & green card
  • Birth & marriage certificates
  • Previous USCIS notices
  • Government ID

Financial & Employment

  • Tax returns (3-5 years)
  • W-2s, pay stubs
  • Employment letters
  • Bank statements

Specific requirements vary by form. We provide a personalized checklist during consultation.
