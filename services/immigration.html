Frequently Asked Questions

Are you immigration attorneys?

No. We provide administrative assistance with USCIS forms and document organization. For legal advice or representation, we refer licensed attorneys.

Which forms do you help with?

N-400, I-130, I-485, I-765, I-90, I-131 and more. We confirm the right forms for your situation in a free consultation.

How long does the process take?

USCIS timelines vary by case and field office. We help you file quickly and track your case, but we cannot control USCIS processing times.

Do you offer translation and notary?

Yes. We coordinate certified translations and notarization as needed so your package is complete.
