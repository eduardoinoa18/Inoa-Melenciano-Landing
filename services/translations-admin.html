Our Translation & Administrative Services

Document Translation

What we translate: Birth certificates, diplomas, transcripts, marriage certificates, divorce decrees, police clearances, medical records, contracts, leases, business documents.

Types: General translation (everyday use) or Certified translation (USCIS, court, official submission).

Languages: English ↔ Spanish

Turnaround: 1-5 business days depending on length

Form Completion Assistance

Forms we help with: Government applications, school enrollment, health insurance, financial aid (FAFSA), rental applications, job applications, benefit enrollment.

What we do: Explain requirements, gather needed documents, complete forms accurately, review before submission.

Duration: 30 min - 2 hours per form

Digital Account Setup

Accounts we help with: Email (Gmail, Outlook), online banking, bill pay services, social media, job boards (Indeed, LinkedIn), government portals (IRS, SSA, USCIS).

What we do: Create accounts, set up security, explain navigation, configure settings, test functionality.

Duration: 30-60 minutes per account

Administrative Support

Tasks we handle: Appointment scheduling, phone call assistance (3-way with interpreter), correspondence drafting, document organization, filing systems, receipt tracking, vendor communication.

Ideal for: Small business owners, self-employed individuals, busy professionals.

Availability: Hourly or package rates

Bookkeeping & Receipt Organization

Services: Categorize expenses, organize receipts (digital or physical), prepare for tax preparer, reconcile bank statements, track mileage.

Tools we use: QuickBooks, Wave, Excel, or your preferred system.

Frequency: Weekly, monthly, or tax season

Communication Support

What we do: Translate emails, draft professional correspondence, interpret phone calls, accompany to appointments (medical, legal, financial), explain complex letters/notices.

Languages: Full bilingual support in English & Spanish.

Scheduling: By appointment

Step-by-Step Process: How We Work With You

1

Service Request & Document Submission

What happens: You contact us (phone, email, or form) describing what you need. For translations, you submit the document via email, WhatsApp, or in-person drop-off.

What you provide: Clear scan or photo of document, deadline, intended use (USCIS, court, personal, etc.).

Response time: Same day (usually within 2 hours during business hours)

2

Quote & Timeline Confirmation

What happens: We review the document/request and provide pricing based on word count, complexity, urgency. We confirm delivery date and any special requirements.

Pricing factors: Document length, technical terminology, certification needed, rush service.

Typical range: $25-$150 for most documents; $30-$75/hour for admin work

3

Translation / Service Completion

What happens: We complete the translation or administrative task. For translations, we maintain formatting, verify accuracy, and ensure cultural appropriateness.

For certified translations: We include certification statement with translator credentials and seal.

Duration: 1-5 business days (standard); same-day/rush available

4

Quality Review & Client Approval

What happens: We review our work for accuracy and completeness. You receive a draft/preview to approve. We make any requested corrections at no extra charge.

What you do: Review the translation/completed work, ask questions, request clarifications.

Revisions: Included in the initial price (within reason)

5

Final Delivery & Certification

What happens: We deliver the final document via your preferred method—email (PDF), printed copy, certified mail for official submissions.

For certified translations: Includes original translation + certification page + translator seal.

Follow-up: We're available for questions if submission entity requests clarification
