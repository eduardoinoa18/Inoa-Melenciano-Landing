    Credit Utilization

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can you guarantee a score increase?

    No. Results depend on your credit profile and creditor/bureau responses. We focus on education and process.

    How long does it take?

    Many clients work through a plan over 3–9 months, depending on number of items and responsiveness.

    Do you send disputes on my behalf?

    We provide templates and guidance. You remain in control and submit disputes directly to creditors/bureaus.

    Should I close old accounts?

    Usually no. Older accounts help credit history length. We explain best practices for your situation.
