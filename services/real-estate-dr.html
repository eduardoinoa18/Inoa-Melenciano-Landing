Frequently Asked Questions

Can I buy in the DR remotely?

Yes. With legal support and proper documentation (including POA when needed), you can complete reservations and closings remotely.

Are foreigners allowed to own property?

Yes. Foreign ownership is permitted in the Dominican Republic. We introduce you to experienced legal partners to ensure compliance.

What are typical closing costs?

They vary by project and province, and can include taxes and legal fees. We outline expected costs and timelines before commitment.

Do you help with property management?

Yes. Many vetted projects offer in-house rental programs or we connect you with trusted managers.
