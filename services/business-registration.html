1

Entity Selection Consultation

What happens: We discuss your business goals, revenue projections, liability concerns, and tax situation to recommend the best entity type.

What you need: Business description, estimated annual revenue, number of owners, growth plans.

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Cost: Free consultation

2

Business Name Check & Reservation

What happens: We search state databases to ensure your desired business name is available. We can reserve it for 60 days (MA) while preparing documents.

What you need: 2-3 preferred business names, preferred domain name (we check availability).

Duration: Same day to 2 business days

Cost: $30-50 (state fee)

3

Articles of Organization/Incorporation

What happens: We prepare and file formation documents with the Secretary of State—Articles of Organization (LLC) or Articles of Incorporation (Corp).

What you need: Business name, registered agent address, member/shareholder names, management structure preference.

Duration: 2-5 business days (expedited available)

Cost: $500 LLC / $275 Corp (MA state filing fee) + service fee

4

EIN Application (Federal Tax ID)

What happens: We complete IRS Form SS-4 to obtain your Employer Identification Number (EIN) needed for banking, taxes, hiring employees.

What you need: Social Security Number, formation documents, business address, intended business activity.

Duration: Same day (online) or 2-4 weeks (mail)

Cost: Free (IRS doesn't charge) + service fee

5

Operating Agreement / Bylaws

What happens: We draft internal governance documents (Operating Agreement for LLC, Bylaws for Corp) outlining ownership, management, profit distribution, decision-making.

What you need: Ownership percentages, management roles, voting rights preferences, profit distribution agreement.

Duration: 3-5 business days

Cost: Included in service package

6

State Tax & License Registration

What happens: We register with MA Department of Revenue for state tax ID, sales tax permit (if applicable), and identify industry-specific licenses needed.

What you need: EIN, formation documents, business description, projected sales.

Duration: 1-2 weeks

Cost: Varies by license type

7

Compliance & Ongoing Support Setup

What happens: We explain annual reporting requirements, set reminders for key deadlines, coordinate with our tax team for proper accounting setup.

What you need: Preferred accounting method, banking information, questions about tax obligations.

Duration: 1 hour consultation

Cost: Included, ongoing compliance available

Ongoing Compliance Requirements

Massachusetts LLC Requirements

  • Annual Report filing (due by Anniversary of formation)
  • $500 filing fee each year
  • Registered agent maintenance
  • Operating Agreement updates as needed
  • Business name renewal if reserved

Corporation Requirements

  • Annual Report + $125 fee
  • Corporate meeting minutes
  • Stock certificates & ledger
  • Board resolutions for major decisions
  • S-Corp election (Form 2553) if applicable

Tax & Financial Obligations

  • Quarterly estimated tax payments (if applicable)
  • Annual federal tax return (1120, 1120-S, or 1065)
  • State tax returns
  • Sales tax filing (if registered)
  • Payroll tax deposits (if employees)

Best Practices

  • Separate business bank account
  • Business credit card
  • Bookkeeping system (QuickBooks, Wave)
  • General liability insurance
  • Professional licenses (if required by industry)
