Tax Prep FAQ

What documents do I need?

Common items: W-2, 1099s, prior-year return, receipts for deductions, and ID. We provide a checklist.

Do you help self-employed or LLC owners?

Yes. We organize books, estimate quarterlies, and maximize deductions for freelancers and small businesses.

Can you assist with IRS letters?

Absolutely. We respond quickly with audit-ready documentation and help resolve notices.

Do you prepare multi-state returns?

Yes. We prepare MA/NH and can coordinate additional states as needed; additional fees may apply.

Can you amend past returns?

Yes. We review your prior return, identify issues, and prepare the amendment if appropriate.

How do I send my documents?

Use our secure portal or encrypted upload link. We’ll send you a clear checklist.

Do you provide legal tax advice?

We provide tax preparation and guidance. For complex legal matters, we may refer you to a tax attorney.
